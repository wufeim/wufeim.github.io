Experiment: Baseline Models

We investigate the performance of various model architectures on CIFAR-10:

  • LeNet
  • AlexNet: not a good baseline model on CIFAR-10 due to 32x32 inputs.
  • VGG: VGG-11, VGG-13, VGG-16, VGG-19
  • ResNet: ResNet-20, ResNet-32, ResNet-44, ResNet-56
Results - LeNet

  1. We achieve very small improvements by training a series of born-again networks (BAN). However, similar performance may be achieved by training multiple times with different initializations.
